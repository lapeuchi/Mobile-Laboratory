--- Planet Earth cross sections ---

Select first the package to extract according to the Scriptable Rendering Pipeline you are using. 
You will find them in the folder named "-- FILES TO EXTRACT HERE --"

The is only one script used in the package : "DrawLine.cs" 
You will find it attached to the "Target" component in the prefabs.
It's only purpose is to link an "origin" prefab to a "target" prefab and draw a line in between.

The shader for the info bubbles has been made by amplify and is editable.
However, even without the Amplify editor, there are open properties you can modify in the material.
